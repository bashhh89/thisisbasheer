## Overview

Built for a national venue-services operation that had outgrown its coordination habits. Three operations managers were running roughly 150 technicians across 43+ venues in 15 markets — out of a shared calendar, spreadsheets, and phone calls.

## The system

Four layers, each feeding the next:

- An **event and staffing engine** — 250+ events per season sync in automatically and get staffed in one view
- A **three-stage mobile field workflow** — check-in, game-ready, post-game report — run by technicians at the venue
- **Ticketing and per-venue documentation** — issues become tracked items with owners; venue knowledge stops living in one person's head
- An **AI reporting layer** — daily digests, escalation alerts, post-game summaries, and a weekly operations report, generated from live data

## What changed

The question "are we covered tonight?" became a glance instead of a phone tree. Operational issues get owners the same night they happen. The office and the field read the same reality, synced with the company CRM on a fixed cadence.

## Final outcome

Three operations managers now run a national field operation from one screen. The platform replaced four weekly reports, a shared calendar, and a culture of phone calls — with a system the team actually works in.
