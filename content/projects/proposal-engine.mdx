## Overview

ANC needed to stop running its sales motion out of spreadsheets. Quotes were being assembled by hand for every opportunity, pricing drifted between reps, and there was no system of record once a proposal was sent.

## The system

The platform is built around three layers:

- A **structured intake** that captures the shape of each opportunity in a way the engine can act on.
- A **catalog** of services, packages, and pricing rules — versioned and centrally managed.
- A **generator** that assembles proposals from intake + catalog, with consistent language and clean output.

Everything lives in one Postgres-backed system. There is no copy-paste between tools.

## Key capabilities

- Configurable pricing rules per service line
- Versioned templates so sent proposals stay reproducible
- Role-based access for sales, ops, and finance
- Client-ready PDF and shareable links
- Audit trail for every change

## What changed

The team stopped negotiating with their own spreadsheets. Reps spend their time on the customer instead of the document. Finance trusts the numbers because they come from one place. Ops sees what's been promised before it lands in their queue.

## Final outcome

ANC now operates with a real proposal system. Onboarding a new offering is a configuration change, not a rebuild. The same engine is the foundation for everything that comes next — contracts, fulfillment handoffs, and the customer portal.
