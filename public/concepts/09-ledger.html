I Build Systems That Run Companies

By Ahmad Basheer · Writing From Production

Most software is handed over and abandoned. The Ledger reports a different practice: platforms that are designed, built, deployed, and then operated by the same person who wrote them — every day, in production, under real load.

What follows is the record. Proposals that took days now take under an hour. Four separate reports were retired in favor of one screen. Sixteen dashboards report live numbers instead of last month's spreadsheet. The details are filed below, with figures.
